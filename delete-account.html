ChuchuConnect Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their ChuchuConnect account and associated data.

How to request deletion

Email us at chuchuconnect@gmail.com from the email address used for your ChuchuConnect account.

Please include:

  • Your ChuchuConnect account email
  • A request to delete your account

What data may be deleted

  • Account profile information
  • Community posts and comments where technically possible
  • Uploaded profile images or community images where technically possible

Processing time

We will review and process deletion requests within 30 days.

Contact

Email: chuchuconnect@gmail.com
