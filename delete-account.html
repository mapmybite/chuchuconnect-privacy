ChuchuConnect Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your ChuchuConnect account and associated data at any time.

How to request account deletion

Option 1 (Recommended)

  1. Sign in to the ChuchuConnect app.
  2. Open your Profile page.
  3. Scroll to the bottom.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your deletion request.

Your request will be reviewed and processed by the ChuchuConnect team.

Option 2

If you cannot access the app, email us at chuchuconnect@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account.

Please include:

  • Your ChuchuConnect account email
  • A request to delete your account

Data that may be deleted

  • Profile information
  • Parent profile details
  • Community posts
  • Comments
  • Uploaded profile images
  • Community images where technically possible
  • Community messages and related account information where technically possible

Data that may be retained

Limited information may be retained when required for legal obligations, fraud prevention, abuse prevention, security investigations, or recordkeeping. Any retained information is kept only as long as reasonably necessary.

Processing time

Most deletion requests are processed within 30 days.
